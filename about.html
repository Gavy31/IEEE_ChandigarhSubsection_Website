About Us

The Chandigarh Subsection of IEEE Delhi Section was formed with Dr. Chepuri S. Rao, SMIEEE as its Interim Chair on June 18, 2005. Subsequently with him as regular Chairperson Executive Committee of Subsection was constituted. It is one of the most well-established and uniquely built subsections whose roots fall under the IEEE Delhi Section. It's known for its well-established connection and network with multiple student branches guided and mentored by it. Exercises coordinated by IEEE Chandigarh Subsection are focal center point for specialized activity during the organizations from Hackathons for engineers and coders to Design their Web, circuit journey and Light it up for electrical and electronic nerds, see incredible interest. The vision to overcome peak, energy to learn, and the collaboration among the individuals has been continually determined individuals forward and energized the growing personalities at Chandigarh subsection to improve for better great of society and succeed in all fields.
