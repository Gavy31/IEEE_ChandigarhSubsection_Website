21-22 June 2022

IEEE R10 EAC's Best Practices in Education

The Punjab Engineering College (Deemed to be University), in collaboration with IEEE Region 10, IEEE Chandigarh SubSection, Galgotias University and IEEE Delhi Section, hosted a "Best Practices in Educational Activities Workshop" ideated by IEEE R-10 Education Activities Committee. Two panel discussions were organised on the themes: Best Practices & Challenges in Higher Education: A Global Perspective and IEEE & Education . The first discussion was moderated by Prof. R Jadeja, Treasurer IEEE R10 and was focused mainly on industry-acadmia collaboration. The key takeaways for the volunteers and delegates were the need of collaboration and Innovation for growth, "Learn, unlearn and relearn", need to shift from being Job seekers to Job providers and the importance of technical teaching. The second panel discussion was presided over by Director IIIT Bangalore, Past Chair IEEE India Council, Former Director IIT Delhi discussed IEEE's future plans and workshops to emphasize technical education.

11-17 July 2022

Advanced Workshop On Semiconductor Design

Punjab Engineering College, in technical collaboration with IEEE Chandigarh Subsection and ISSE Chandigarh Chapter,inaugurated the Advanced workshop on Semiconductor Device, Design, Fabrication and Characterization. The lab focused on importance of Semiconductor in our daily lives, how esteemed institution will help the nation towards the national aim of in-house semiconductor manufacturing and development unit. This workshop is the first step towards the government initiative of “Accelerate Vigyan” under KARYASHALA Scheme. Dr. Jatinder K. Arora, Executive Director of PSCST focused on the importance of these workshops and how the initiative by department of ECE, PEC will help our young researchers in the future innovations. She emphasized on the timeline for drafting of proposal in collaborative manner and promised strong delivery in the coming year.

TECHADROIT

A Student Branch of IEEE PEC presents the biggest and most admirable event of the year, Techadroit 22. 1st October 2021

DESIGNOVATION

IEEE PEC Student Branch provides a platform for web-dev enthusiasts to show their skills off and enhance their web-development skills. Designovation was a competition wherein the participants needed to design a minimalistic frontend working application according to the given problem statement. The competition comprised of two rounds. For round 1, they had to choose a problem statement from a given set of problem statements and propose a web based solution. In round 2, they had to push their code on GitHub and present the solution in ppt and real time working.
